About

Introducing @0lycm_ – A Visual Storyteller of Emotion, Fandom, and Passion. @0lycm_ is a dedicated digital and traditional artist whose work bridges heartfelt emotion and fan-driven inspiration. Specializing in fanart tribute to movies, TV shows, and beloved characters, each piece carries deep emotional resonance.

Their recent painting inspired by the sci-fi thriller Companion gained recognition from stars Jack Quaid and Sophie Thatcher. Jack Quaid shared the artwork on his Instagram story, marking an important milestone.

@0lycm_ also explores traditional media through expressive portraits of celebrities and musical artists, capturing emotional snapshots beyond mere likeness.

"Do what you like, and you will be at peace."
